Why me?

Certified. Industry Active

I don't just know careers. I'm living one.

I'm Keshav — a Certified Global Career Counsellor credentialed through UCLA Extension and the Univariety GCC Green Belt program. I work with students from Class 5 to 12 to help them understand themselves before the world rushes them into a decision.

But here's what makes this different.

Most guidance stops at assessments and advice. Mine is grounded in something most counsellors don't have — active, current industry experience. At ION Group, I've moved across the full spectrum: product support, automation engineering, tech-business consulting, client relationship management, and delivery. I became the youngest Delivery Manager in the organisation — not by tenure, but by performance.

That lived trajectory shapes every session I hold.

When I help a student map their interests and strengths, I'm not working from a textbook. I'm thinking like someone who has been inside the careers we're discussing. When a student sits between Science and Commerce, or a Class 5 child needs a head start in building confidence and clarity — I bring both the psychological framework and the professional reality to that conversation.

My approach combines psychology-based assessments, holistic development frameworks, and genuine one-on-one conversation. Every student gets individual attention — not a copy-pasted report, not borrowed knowledge from a decade ago.

Globally certified. Industry-active. Genuinely invested in your child's future.
